We propose a novel extrapolation approach to uniqueness of weak solutions for a wide class of transport equations. In particular, this unifies and extends the classical works of Yudovich and Vishik on 2D Euler equations for incompressible fluid flows with associated nearly bounded and BMO vorticities, respectively.
